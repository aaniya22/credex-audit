# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Assume:
- Average deal size: $2,000 in credits purchased
- Gross margin on credits: ~30% = $600 gross profit per customer
- Average customer buys credits 3x before churning = $1,800 LTV

Conservative estimate: **$600–$1,800 LTV per converted customer**

## CAC by Channel

| Channel | Est. CAC | Basis |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Hacker News Show HN | ~$0 | Organic, time cost only |
| Reddit posts | ~$0 | Organic |
| X cold DMs | ~$5 | Time cost ~15min per conversion |
| Paid social (future) | ~$80–150 | Industry benchmark for B2B SaaS |

## Conversion Funnel

| Stage | Rate | Basis |
|-------|------|-------|
| Visitor → Audit completed | 25% | Low friction, no login |
| Audit completed → Email captured | 20% | Value shown before ask |
| Email captured → Consultation booked | 10% | Only high-savings cases shown CTA |
| Consultation → Credit purchase | 30% | Warm lead with proven savings |

**Overall: 1,000 visitors → 250 audits → 50 emails → 5 consultations → 1.5 purchases**

At $600 gross profit: **1,000 visitors = ~$900 gross profit**
CAC at organic channels: effectively $0 → very high ROI

## What Makes This Profitable

The tool itself costs almost nothing to run:
- Supabase free tier: $0
- Vercel free tier: $0  
- Resend free tier: $0 (up to 3,000 emails/month)
- Anthropic API: ~$0.01 per summary = $10 per 1,000 audits

Break-even: **1 credit purchase per month** covers all costs.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

Assumptions:
- Average revenue per customer: $2,000 (credits purchased)
- $1M ARR = 500 customers
- 18 months to get there

Month 1–3: 100 audits/month, 0.15% visitor→purchase = 0 purchases (building awareness)
Month 4–6: 1,000 audits/month, 1.5 purchases/month = 9 purchases = $18,000
Month 7–12: 5,000 audits/month, 7.5 purchases/month = 45 purchases = $90,000
Month 13–18: 20,000 audits/month, 30 purchases/month = 180 purchases = $360,000

**What has to be true:**
- HN + Reddit drives consistent organic traffic (achievable with 1 viral post)
- Consultation → purchase rate holds at 30% (requires good sales process)
- Average deal size stays at $2,000 (requires Credex to have inventory)
- Tool stays free and frictionless (no login wall kills the funnel)
